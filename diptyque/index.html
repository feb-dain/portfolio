ARTIST EDITIONS

  • Rabih Kayrouz
  • Johan Creten
  • Zoë Paul

Maison rabih kayrouz weaves its vision with creativity and integrity to produce a serene
and generous interpretation of “luxury”. Everything we do traces back to the gesture
of the hand. Encouraged by his parents, kayrouz at the age of 16 moves to paris
to attend preparation school, enrolling at the chambre syndicale de la couture parisienne.

“Paris was my first big city. The streets and style of that time made a big impression on me.”

The first artist is one of the greatest contemporary sculptors. He’s a pioneer in the innovative
use of ceramics and the first Belgian artist to be honoured at the Louvre Museum.

His name is Johan Creten. The choice to give him Venice, a city much loved and visited by
diptyque’s founders, was an obvious one. Famous for his large allegorical bronze sculptures,
the Paris-based artist is a lover of nature, the art of perfume and Venetian bronzes.

Zoë Paul is a South African-born, Athens-based sculptor and painter.
She combines traditional techniques with products of industrial waste
to examine community, craft and domestic spaces.

Her work is particularly inspired by blurred lines between the interior and the exterior.
As Paul is a trained sculptor, her art has also been influenced by Classical Greek sculpture.

FAQ

How do I inquire about Corporate Sales?

Please complete our Corporate Sales form and submit your inquiry.
Please note that no VAT exemption will be granted for online orders.
We invite you to contact us for any complementary information.

How can I locate my UPS tracking number?

When your package ships, you will receive an e-mail with your tracking
information to the address entered at the time of the order.
If you do not receive this, please contact Customer Care.

Can I select the date/time for delivery?

Delivery dates and times are not able to be specified at this time.
A delivery window will be set by UPS once the package ships
and can be found using the tracker.

How do I update the shipping or billing address on my order?

The billing address on an order is unable to be updated after the order
has been processed. Rest assured, we do not include a receipt within the parcel
and it will only be sent to the email entered at the time of the order.

DELIVERY & GIFT WRAPPING

How long does it take to process an order?

All orders received before 11:00 a.m. BST Monday to Friday (except holidays) will process
within 24 business hours - often the same day. Orders received after 11:00 a.m. BST,
on weekends or holidays will process the next business day. Final delivery will be
within 2-5 business days of order.

Can I have my order gift wrapped?

Select gift wrap by checking the appropriate box in our customer checkout and entering
a gift message. Each order is wrapped in a signature diptyque gift box and is accompanied
with a gift card. At this time we do not offer per item gift wrapping. Should you require
separate gift wrapping, please place separate orders.
